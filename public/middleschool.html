Middle School Program

Navigating the pivotal years of adolescence with academic challenge, social-emotional support, and personal growth opportunities.

Apply Now

Developing Confident Learners

Our Middle School program serves students in Grades 6-8 during a critical period of intellectual, social, and emotional development. We provide a challenging academic curriculum while supporting students through the transitions of adolescence.

Our program emphasizes critical thinking, collaboration, and communication skills while allowing students to explore their interests through electives and extracurricular activities. We foster independence while providing the structure and support middle school students need to thrive.

With dedicated advisors, project-based learning, and a focus on developing executive functioning skills, we prepare students for the increased demands of high school and beyond.

Our Middle School Curriculum

English Language Arts

Students develop advanced reading comprehension, analytical writing, and presentation skills through literature studies, research projects, and public speaking opportunities.

Mathematics

Our math program progresses from pre-algebra to algebra and geometry, emphasizing problem-solving strategies and real-world applications of mathematical concepts.

Science

Students explore earth science, life science, and physical science through hands-on laboratories, scientific inquiry, and developing research skills.

Social Studies

Our curriculum covers world geography, ancient civilizations, and American history, helping students understand historical context and global interconnectedness.

Balanced Approach to Middle School Education

Our Middle School program is designed to address the unique needs of early adolescents through a balanced approach that challenges students academically while supporting their social and emotional development.

Key elements of our approach include:

  • Departmentalized instruction with subject-specific teachers
  • Project-based learning that connects concepts across disciplines
  • Differentiated instruction to meet diverse learning needs
  • Explicit instruction in study skills and organization
  • Regular communication between teachers, students, and families

Exploratory Electives

Middle School students have the opportunity to explore their interests through a variety of elective courses that rotate throughout the year.

Digital Media & Design

Students learn graphic design, video production, and digital storytelling using industry-standard software and equipment.

Robotics & Engineering

Hands-on experience with building and programming robots while learning engineering principles and design thinking.

Creative Writing

Students develop their unique voice through poetry, short stories, and personal narratives in a supportive workshop environment.

World Languages

Introduction to Spanish and French with focus on basic conversation, cultural awareness, and language foundations.

Advisory Program

Every Middle School student is part of a small advisory group that meets daily with a faculty advisor. This program provides academic support, social-emotional learning, and community building.

Advisory activities include:

  • Goal setting and progress monitoring
  • Organization and time management skill development
  • Social skills and conflict resolution
  • Community service projects
  • Preparation for student-led conferences

Advisors serve as the primary point of contact for families and provide consistent support throughout the middle school years.

Middle School Activities
